Our Story

Building Memorable Experiences Every Day

BBRF Club House was established with a vision of creating a world-class destination where residents, visitors, families, friends, and corporate organizations can enjoy quality hospitality services in a peaceful and secure environment. Our commitment is to provide luxurious accommodation, excellent dining experiences, exciting recreational activities, professional event services and outstanding customer care. Every guest is welcomed with warmth, respect and a dedication to excellence that makes every visit truly memorable. Today, BBRF Club House continues to grow as one of the preferred relaxation destinations within NAF Valley Estate, offering facilities that meet international hospitality standards while maintaining a friendly and welcoming atmosphere.

Our Vision
